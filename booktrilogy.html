Book Trilogy Design

Short Description of the Project

I was tasked with designing book covers for three of David Sedaris's ingenious short stories. I began with designing the first book cover for the story Untamed, which was on making friends with animals. I then repeated my creative process to create the second and third book covers, considering cohesiveness and viability for a complete trilogy.
