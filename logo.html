Logo Design

Short Description of the Project

I was tasked to create a tourism logo for an international city that embodies its spirit, arts, culture, history, and present. I chose Jaipur. Jaipur became known as "The Pink City" when, in 1876, Maharaja Ram Singh had all royal and official buildings painted pink in preparation for the visit of the Prince of Wales and Britain's Queen Victoria. Today, the city is known for its bazaars, forts, palaces, temples, and wildlife sanctuaries.
